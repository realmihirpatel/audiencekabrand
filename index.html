About Audience Ka Brand

Audience Ka Brand is a community-first entertainment brand created by a passionate YouTuber and movie lover. The mission is simple: Bring together cinema lovers, wrestling fans, cricket fans and theatre enthusiasts through content, merchandise and unforgettable fan experiences. From theatre visits and vlogs to premium oversized apparel, everything is made by the audience and for the audience.
